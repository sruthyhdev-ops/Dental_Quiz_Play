Privacy Policy for Dental Exam Quiz
Last Updated: March 29, 2026

1. Introduction
Welcome to Dental Exam Quiz, a mobile application developed by ZMOHI. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and disclose information when you use our app.

2. Information We Collect
Account Information: We use Supabase Authentication. When you sign up via Google or Email, we collect your Email Address and a unique User ID to manage your access.

Subscription Data: We store your subscription status (Start and End dates) in our secure database to provide access to premium features like the Annual Pass.

Usage Data: All quiz scores and progress are stored locally on your device. We do not sync or store your quiz performance on our servers. If you delete the app, your progress will be reset.

Device Identifiers: We use Google AdMob to serve advertisements. AdMob may collect and use your device’s advertising ID to personalize ads for you.

3. Third-Party Services
We utilize trusted third-party services to ensure a smooth experience:

Supabase: For secure authentication and account database management.

RevenueCat: To manage and validate your subscription status.

Google AdMob: To provide advertisements within the free version of the app.

Google Play Billing: To process your Annual Pass transactions. We never see or store your financial details.

4. Data Security
We take security seriously. All data sent between the app and our servers is encrypted in transit using HTTPS/TLS protocols.

5. Data Deletion & Retention
Retention: In accordance with our "Fresh Database" policy, we periodically delete data for accounts that have been inactive or have had expired subscriptions for more than one year.

Deletion Requests: You have the right to request the deletion of your account and all associated data. To initiate this, please email us at contact_zmohi@aol.com. We will process and confirm your request within 14 business days.
